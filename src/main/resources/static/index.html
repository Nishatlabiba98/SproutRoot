Why SproutRoot?
"I grew up watching children learn beautiful things through gray pencils and bare white paper, and I never forgot that frustration. When I became a mother, I chose Montessori — because real learning happens through touch, movement, and independence. When my scattered activity notes and lesson plans were not working, I realized the idea deserved more — and SproutRoot became my answer: a Montessori-research-backed learning app built from scratch."
🕊️ Maria Montessori believed
"The child leads, the environment teaches, and the hands educate the mind. SproutRoot is that belief in a parent-led game app — a wooden shelf instead of a worksheet, a chime instead of a red mark, and a parent who observes instead of corrects."

The Three Period Lesson
David Gettman's Basic Montessori
"The Three Period Lesson works because it mirrors how the toddler brain actually builds memory — encoding first, retrieval through action second, and verbal recall only when the pathway is strong enough to support it. Rushing to 'What is this?' before the concept is consolidated causes anxiety and shuts learning down — common in flashcard apps. SproutRoot follows all three periods every session: Period 1 introduces, Period 2 lets Nishan show without pressure, and the game itself is Period 3 — independent recall through play."
1
This is...
The concept is introduced with sight, sound, and touch simultaneously. Three sensory channels fire at once — creating a richer memory trace.
2
Show me...
The child identifies through action — tapping, pointing, or doing — with no verbal pressure and no wrong answer feedback. Motor retrieval strengthens the neural pathway.
3
What is this?
Only now does the child attempt independent verbal recall — when the pathway is strong enough. This is the game itself. Every question round is Period 3.
